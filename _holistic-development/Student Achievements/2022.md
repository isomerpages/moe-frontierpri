<h4><u>Term 1</u></h4>
<p><strong>13th Global Village International Children and Youths Bilingual Speech Competition</strong>
<br><strong>(Singapore Station)</strong>
</p>
<p>The 13th “Global Village” Children &amp; Youths Bilingual Speech Competition
was organized by Goodtalk Culture Communication Co. Ltd, sponsored by China
Education Association for International Exchange (CEAIE) and Goodtalk Youths
&amp; Children Cultural Communication Center.</p>
<p>The aim of the activity is to provide a chance for students in Singapore
and China to study Chinese and English. In the competition, students could
learn Chinese and English through the cooperation and competition, and
they could know each other’s culture better. At the same time, the competition
cooperate with the promotion of “Speak Chinese Language Activity” in Singapore.
Students in Singapore could learn to speak Chinese language through the
expression of speech.</p>

<h4><u>Term 2</u></h4>
<p><strong>NUS High School Oratorical Challenge 2021</strong>
<br>Oratorical Challenge 2021 was organised by the NUS High School of Mathematics
and Science. The competition aims to develop participants’ versatility
in oral communication, hone their skills in research and speech delivery,
as well as cultivate interest in scientific research and public speaking.</p>
<p>23 primary schools in Singapore participated in this competition and it
was adapted to be in the form of video submission due to the Safe Management
Measures requirements in 2021.</p>
