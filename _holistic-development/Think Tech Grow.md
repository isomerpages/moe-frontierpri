<p><strong>Think .Tech .Grow </strong>is a whole-school teaching and learning
framework that empowers teachers to cultivate future-ready learners and <strong><u>enables the holistic development of E21CC and student outcomes</u>.</strong>
</p>
<p>• &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Think</strong>: <strong>Use thinking strategies</strong> to
strengthen students’ cognitive skills and learning capacity.</p>
<p>• &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Tech</strong>: <strong>Harness technology and EdTech PS </strong>to
design effective active learning experiences.</p>
<p>• &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong>Grow</strong>: <strong>Apply assessment literacy </strong>to
deepen students’ learning and foster development of future-ready competencies
(e.g. digital literacy, social-emotional skills, and E21CC).</p>
