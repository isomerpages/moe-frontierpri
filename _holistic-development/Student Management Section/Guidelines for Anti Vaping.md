<p>Frontier Primary School adopts a strict <strong>zero-tolerance</strong> stance
against vaping. Any student found vaping or in possession of vaping devices
will be deemed to have committed <strong><u>a serious offence</u></strong> and
will be subjected to disciplinary action, in accordance with school rules
and national regulations.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Consequences on Vaping</strong>
<br>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Student offenders will be subjected to national
penalties imposed by Health Science Authority (HSA) as seen from Tables
1A and 1B.</p>
