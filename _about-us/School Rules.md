<p>Students of Frontier Primary School must uphold the good name of the school
and abide by the school rules.
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>SINGING OF NATIONAL ANTHEM &amp; PLEDGE TAKING</strong>
</p>
<p>• Students who are Singapore Citizens must sing the National Anthem and
take the Pledge.
<br>• Students will take the Pledge with the right fist placed over the heart.</p>
<p><strong>General Appearance and School Attire</strong>
<br>• Students are to wear the prescribed school uniform and any modification
to the uniform is not allowed.
<br>• Students must be neat in appearance and boys are to be clean-shaven.</p>
<p><strong>GENERAL CONDUCT</strong>
<br>• Students should uphold the core values of the school.
<br>• Students should sing the school song with pride.
<br>• Students should be respectful, courteous and well-behaved at all times.
<br>• Students should greet the teachers, staff and visitors when they see
them.
<br>• Students should be orderly and self-disciplined at all times.
<br>• Students should be punctual for school and all school activities.
<br>• Students should respect school property.
<br>• Students should complete and submit all assignments punctually.
<br>• Students should be attentive at all times.
<br>• Students are not allowed to have in their possession any weapon. They
are also not allowed to bring any weapon-like item which can be used or
intended to be used to cause harm to others.</p>
<p><strong>CLASSROOM CONDUCT</strong>
<br>• Students must seek permission from the teacher before entering and leaving
the classroom.
<br>• Students should maintain the cleanliness of the school environment.
<br>• Students should take care of their own belongings.
<br>• Students should have integrity in words and deeds.</p>
