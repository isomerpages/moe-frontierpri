<p><strong>CCA Information:</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Rockwall Climbing CCA started in 2017 with the objectives of providing
students with fundamental skills in climbing as well as enabling them to
develop strategies and techniques of rockwall climbing. It also aims to
cultivate important values and life skills such as self-discipline, leadership
and good sportsmanship whilst providing them with a tactile and fun experience.</p>
