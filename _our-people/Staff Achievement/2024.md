<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Redesigning Pedagogy International Conference</strong>
</p>
<p>RPIC 2024 brought together educators, researchers, and thought leaders
from around the globe to explore innovative teaching practices and transformative
learning experiences. The theme for 2024 focused on harnessing technology,
creativity, and collaboration to meet the evolving needs of learners in
a dynamic world. 3 groups of teachers from Frontier Primary were selected
to share their research.</p>
